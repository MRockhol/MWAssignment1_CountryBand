About the Band

Beginnings

Boots 'N Beer first came together unintentionally on stage for karaoke in small bar in Des Moines. After performing, they found that they matched fairly well and had the makings of an actual band. Starting with small gigs, they began making headway with Boots 'N Beer when a scout heard some of their music and got them spots opening for more popular bands performing in the Des Moines area.

If you think you have seen the best country band you have ever seen already in your life, you are wrong! Get your tickets and come see Boots N Beer at their next performance. If you don't have the greatest experience you have had in your entire life, we guarantee your money back! Also, don't forget to check out our merchandise tents at Boots N Beer performances. We have new and unique products each and every performance!
