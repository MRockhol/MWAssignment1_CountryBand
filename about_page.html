About the Band

Coming together for the first time by singing at a bar's karaoke machine, "Boots 'n Beer" have been making music for just under a year now. In that time frame, they have released two small albums. They will soon be going on a tour in some smaller towns located around Des Moines including:

  • Indianola
  • Ames
  • Ankeny
